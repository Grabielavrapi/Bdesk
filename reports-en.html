Reports and their generation

Standard reports are pre-set reports that are available for all properties, property groups, or individual properties. These reports can be exported in Excel or .csv formats.

How to generate reports

If you manage more than one property, you can choose whether you want to create a report for a specific property or for all properties.

Select the period and the bookings you want to include in the report.
Click on the respective report button to generate it.
If you want to filter the report by specific rooms, you can add a room parameter at the end of the report URL in the browser.
