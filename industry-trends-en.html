Data and Trends in Hospitality & Tourism

Increase in Demand for Direct Bookings

Guests are increasingly preferring direct bookings through hotel websites, avoiding high fees from intermediary platforms and getting more value.

Reduce commission fees and improve customer relationships.
Increase bookings through direct channels.

The use of digital platforms is improving the management of bookings and prices, increasing operational efficiency and reducing errors.

  • Using automation for more efficient management.
  • Real-time price optimization to increase revenue.

Improving the Guest Experience

Personalizing offers and services helps create an unforgettable experience for guests and increase their loyalty. The use of data analytics and technology helps deliver an experience tailored to the individual needs of each customer.

Invest in improving the environment and services.
Use technology for personalizing services.
Improve communication and support for guests.
Offer personalized offers and promotions.
